TmX De’ Luxury Footwear - Site Plan

Site Name

TmX De’ Luxury Footwear

TmX represents the founder’s identity while “De’ Luxury” emphasizes premium craftsmanship. The brand focuses on luxury, comfort, and modern footwear fashion for global customers.

Site Purpose

This website serves as an online footwear marketplace where users can:

  • Browse luxury footwear collections
  • View product prices in multiple currencies
  • Add items to cart and checkout
  • Create accounts and log in
  • Connect through social media platforms

Scenarios

  • Where can I buy premium sneakers and luxury shoes online at affordable global prices?
  • How do I create an account and track my orders on TmX De’ Luxury?

Color Scheme

  • Black (#0b0b0b) – Used for headers, navigation bar, and footer background. It represents luxury and strength.
  • White (#ffffff) – Used for page background and clean layout spacing to improve readability and elegance.
  • Grey (#6e6e6e) – Used for body text, product descriptions, and secondary information.
  • Red Accent (#b30000) – Used sparingly for buttons, highlights, price tags, and important call-to-action elements (e.g., “Add to Cart”).

This color palette is designed to create a modern luxury shopping experience with strong contrast, clean readability, and premium branding appeal.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Headings (luxury branding feel)
  • Poppins – Body text (modern readability)
